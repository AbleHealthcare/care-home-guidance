# Laundry guidance for care homes

Effective laundry processes are essential in care homes to maintain hygiene,
protect residents, and extend the lifespan of textiles.

Common issues include incorrect wash temperatures, overloading machines,
and using unsuitable detergents for large duvets and bedding.
